Virtual Education

As of late 2020, our students returned to campus on a hybrid basis. Each student attends school part-time and needs access to a tablet PC to learn at home on the days in between. Due to the intense vulnerability of our children, a disruption in their education is devastating. We must do what we can. We need to provide each family with a tablet to facilitate virtual learning.
